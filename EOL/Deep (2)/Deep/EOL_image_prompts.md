# EOL System — Before / After Image Prompts (A1 Landscape)

Two dense-but-narrative single-paragraph prompts. Every real-world pain-point and
benefit of the EOL project is woven in — time savings, safety, video evidence,
data accessibility, poka-yoke bypass monitoring, audit trail, remote access —
without becoming a cluttered infographic.

---

## 🟥 PROMPT 1 — BEFORE EOL (soft-red base)

```
Create a wide A1-landscape (7:5, horizontal) photo-realistic editorial
illustration titled "BEFORE — The Manual Era" set on an automotive seat-track
assembly line at Toyota Boshoku in Bawal, India. The whole canvas sits on a
soft brick-red wash — faded, warm, dusty — to express a tired, reactive,
error-prone mood of the pre-digital era. Keep the composition cinematic and
breathable (not a cluttered infographic), with three gentle zones that flow
left to right. In the LEFT zone the shop floor is crowded with human
busywork: an operator squats at his station tally-marking OK/NG parts on a
greasy paper register while a supervisor beside him clicks a mechanical
stopwatch to time one cycle by hand; behind them, a magnetic whiteboard is
smeared with partially-erased plan bars and yesterday's shift numbers still
pending update. Scatter small red paper slips around — leave-forms, reject
tags — hinting at manual dependency, fatigue and lost hours. In the CENTRE
zone show the chaos of missing data and zero traceability: a rejected-parts
bin overflows with red "NG — reason unknown" labels; above it, a poka-yoke
sensor is visibly bypassed with a crude strip of silver tape, and an
operator has his foot on a safety mat with the indicator light disabled —
a quiet hint at risk and cheating that no one can see from outside. A
clunky desktop PC shows an Excel sheet full of red "DATA MISMATCH" cells,
and a junior engineer is frantically retyping numbers from a handwritten
log. A man mid-stride carries a bundle of papers toward a back office,
blurred in motion, conveying delay. In the RIGHT zone, aged 2000s CRT
monitors show a static, day-old dashboard; the supervisor argues on the
phone with a customer, holding a rejected part in his hand but with no
video, no timestamp, no evidence — only his word. A clipboard next to him
reads "Cycle-time measure: 30 min manual · Daily OEE: 2 hr in Excel · Root
cause: 1 day · Video review: not available". Across the top, subtle
hand-stamped red labels read "No real-time OEE · Reports next day · Manual
entry ≈ 12 % error · Bypass invisible · No video evidence · No audit
trail · No remote visibility · Safety relies on trust". Through the top
edge drift soft clouds of red dust. Overall palette: desaturated brick
reds, faded grey metal, yellowing paper, weak fluorescent light. Include
a small Toyota Boshoku logo top-left and the footer
"Production Line: YNC-SS  |  Shift A  |  Era: pre-2026". Render
ultra-sharp, editorial quality, suitable for A1 printing.
```

## 🟩 PROMPT 2 — AFTER EOL (soft-green base)

```
Create a wide A1-landscape (7:5, horizontal) photo-realistic editorial

top-left and the footer "Production Line: YNC-SS  |  Shift A  |  2026".
Render ultra-sharp, editorial quality, suitable for A1 printing.
```

### 💡 Tips

- Midjourney: append `--ar 7:5 --v 6 --style raw`.
- DALL-E 3 / ChatGPT: paste as-is; add "landscape / horizontal".
- For a true A1 print (9933 × 7016 px @ 300 DPI), upscale the output with
  Topaz Gigapixel / waifu2x / an online 4× upscaler.
- Stitch the two outputs side-by-side on an A1 landscape sheet in Canva,
  Figma, or PowerPoint for the final comparison poster.
